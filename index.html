ABOUT US

Welcome to Meteor Hospital, a leading healthcare institution with a remarkable history that traces back to its establishment in 1947. What began as a modest community clinic has evolved into a renowned medical centre for exceptional patient care and groundbreaking medical advancements.


Situated on a sprawling 10-acre campus, Meteor Hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and staffed by highly skilled medical professionals. Our hospital specializes in various medical disciplines, including cardiology, neurology, oncology, orthopaedics, and paediatrics, among others.


At Meteor Hospital, patient well-being is at the heart of everything we do. Our compassionate and dedicated team of doctors, nurses, and staff work tirelessly to ensure that each patient receives personalized attention and the highest quality of care. We understand that every individual is unique, and we tailor our treatment plans to suit the specific needs of each patient.


In addition to our medical expertise, we strive to create a comfortable and welcoming environment for our patients. Our spacious and modern facilities are designed to provide a soothing atmosphere that promotes healing and recovery. From the moment you step through our doors, you will be greeted with warmth and hospitality, making your experience at Meteor Hospital truly exceptional.


As a pioneer in medical research and innovation, Meteor Hospital is committed to advancing healthcare practices and finding new solutions to improve patient outcomes. We actively collaborate with leading research institutions and invest in cutting-edge technologies to stay at the forefront of medical advancements.


Whether you are seeking routine medical care, specialized treatment, or a second opinion, Meteor Hospital is here to meet your healthcare needs. We are dedicated to providing you and your family with the highest standard of medical care, guided by integrity, compassion, and excellence.


Thank you for choosing Meteor Hospital as your healthcare partner. We look forward to serving you and being a part of your journey to a healthier and happier life.
